When a vaccine has an adjuvant added at the time of administration and their lot numbers are not inextricably linked, it may be important to record each component as a separate event. That is, each is recorded in a separate RXA. The vaccine is recorded in one order group (ORC/RXA) and the adjuvant is recorded in a second order group (ORC/RXA).

Example:
